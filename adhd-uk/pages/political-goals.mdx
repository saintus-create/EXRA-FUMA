ADHD UK advocates for policy changes and improved services for people with ADHD across the United Kingdom. Our political goals focus on creating systemic change that benefits the entire ADHD community.

## Our Advocacy Priorities

### Healthcare Access

- Reduce waiting times for ADHD assessment and diagnosis
- Ensure consistent access to ADHD treatment across all regions
- Improve training for healthcare professionals in ADHD
- Support for medication access and affordability

### Education Support

- Better identification and support for students with ADHD
- Training for teachers and school staff
- Appropriate accommodations in educational settings
- Support for transitions between educational stages

### Workplace Rights

- Promote understanding of ADHD in the workplace
- Support for reasonable adjustments
- Protection against discrimination
- Guidance for employers on supporting employees with ADHD

### Research Funding

- Increase funding for ADHD research
- Support longitudinal studies on ADHD across the lifespan
- Promote research into gender differences in ADHD
- Investigate effective interventions and support strategies

## How We Advocate

- Engaging with policymakers and government officials
- Responding to consultations and inquiries
- Building coalitions with other organizations
- Amplifying the voices of people with ADHD
- Providing evidence-based recommendations

## Get Involved

You can support our advocacy efforts by:

- Sharing your experiences with policymakers
- Participating in our campaigns
- Contacting your representatives
- Joining our advocacy network
- Supporting our research initiatives

Together, we can create meaningful change for people with ADHD across the UK.
