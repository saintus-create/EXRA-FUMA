CHADD has always been at the forefront when the ADHD community faced adversity. We continue to step up, meeting these obstacles and the new ones that seem to pop up each day. But we don't do this alone. We need you to step up with your ADHD community and make your voice heard. We are not alone if we act together.

## Our Advocacy Mission

CHADD advocates for policies and practices that improve the lives of individuals affected by ADHD. We work with:

- Federal and state governments
- Healthcare providers
- Educational institutions
- Employers
- Insurance companies

## Key Advocacy Areas

- **Healthcare Access** - Ensuring affordable and accessible ADHD diagnosis and treatment
- **Education Rights** - Protecting educational accommodations and support services
- **Workplace Rights** - Promoting inclusive employment practices
- **Insurance Coverage** - Advocating for comprehensive mental health coverage
- **Research Funding** - Supporting ADHD research initiatives

## How You Can Help

Join CHADD's advocacy efforts by:

- Contacting your representatives
- Sharing your story
- Participating in awareness campaigns
- Supporting legislation that helps the ADHD community
- Joining local advocacy chapters

## ADHD Advocacy Action Center

Visit the [CHADD Advocacy Action Center](https://chadd.org/adhd-advocacy-action-center/) to take action on current issues affecting the ADHD community.

Together, we can make a difference in the lives of millions affected by ADHD.
