## Get in Touch

Have a question, a correction to suggest, or a study to contribute?

**Email:** hello@managedcaresguide.org  
**Response time:** Within 48 hours.

## How to Contribute

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Submit a Correction
    Include the page and section where the error appears, what the current data shows, and the correct data with a supporting source (DOI, FDA document, or guideline URL).
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Contribute a Study
    Provide the full citation (authors, year, journal, DOI), a brief summary of relevance, and the source category: International Research, U.S. LDX Studies, or Clinical Guidelines.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Request an Evidence Brief
    If you are a managed care organization, PBM, or health plan and need a detailed evidence brief for a specific formulary review, reach out with your organization name and clinical question.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Privacy

Information submitted is used only to respond to your inquiry. No personal data is sold or shared.
