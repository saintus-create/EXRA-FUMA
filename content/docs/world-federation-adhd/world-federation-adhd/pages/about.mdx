The World Federation of ADHD is based physically in Germany and serves as a professional association for international researchers, clinicians, and allied healthcare professionals.

## Our Mission

The institution coordinates major international congresses and publishes comprehensive global clinical consensus statements that compile and disseminate top-tier scientific discoveries regarding ADHD.

## What We Do

### International Congresses

The World Federation of ADHD organizes major international congresses that:

- Bring together researchers and clinicians from around the world
- Present the latest ADHD research and clinical findings
- Facilitate international collaboration
- Provide continuing education for professionals
- Showcase innovations in ADHD assessment and treatment

### Global Clinical Consensus Statements

We publish consensus statements that:

- Compile top-tier scientific discoveries
- Establish global standards for ADHD care
- Provide evidence-based guidance for clinicians
- Address gaps in knowledge and practice
- Guide future research directions

### Research Dissemination

The federation works to:

- Disseminate cutting-edge ADHD research globally
- Bridge the gap between research and clinical practice
- Promote evidence-based ADHD care
- Facilitate international research collaboration
- Support the development of new researchers

## Who We Are

Our members include:

- International ADHD researchers
- Clinicians and psychiatrists
- Psychologists and mental health professionals
- Neuroscientists and neurologists
- Allied healthcare professionals
- Academic institutions and research centers

## Why the World Federation Matters

ADHD is a global condition that requires:

- International collaboration in research
- Consensus on best practices
- Sharing of knowledge across borders
- Standardization of diagnostic criteria
- Global understanding of treatment efficacy

The World Federation of ADHD provides the platform for this international collaboration.

## Our Impact

Through our work, we:

- Advance global understanding of ADHD
- Improve clinical practice worldwide
- Support evidence-based treatment
- Foster international research networks
- Promote excellence in ADHD care

## Get Involved

Professionals can join the World Federation of ADHD to:

- Attend international congresses
- Access consensus statements and guidelines
- Network with global ADHD experts
- Contribute to international research
- Stay current with global ADHD developments

For more information, visit the [World Federation of ADHD](https://www.adhd-federation.org/).
