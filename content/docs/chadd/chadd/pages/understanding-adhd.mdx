Understanding ADHD is the first step toward effective management and support. CHADD (Children and Adults with Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder) provides comprehensive resources for individuals, families, and professionals seeking information about ADHD.

## What is ADHD?

Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD) is a neurodevelopmental disorder that affects both children and adults. It is characterized by persistent patterns of inattention, hyperactivity, and impulsivity that interfere with functioning or development.

## Key Information

ADHD affects approximately 5-11% of school-age children and continues into adulthood for many individuals. The disorder impacts various aspects of life including:

- Academic performance
- Work productivity
- Social relationships
- Daily functioning
