ADHD UK relies on the generosity of donors to continue our vital work supporting the ADHD community. Your donation helps us provide information, support, advocacy, and research.

## How Your Donation Helps

### Information and Resources

- Maintaining and updating our website with accurate information
- Creating educational materials for families and professionals
- Providing free resources to those who need them most

### Support Services

- Running support groups across the UK
- Providing helpline services
- Offering training and workshops
- Supporting families navigating diagnosis and treatment

### Advocacy

- Campaigning for better ADHD services
- Engaging with policymakers and healthcare providers
- Responding to government consultations
- Amplifying the voices of people with ADHD

### Research

- Funding important ADHD research studies
- Supporting research into better treatments
- Investigating the long-term impacts of ADHD
- Promoting evidence-based practice

## Ways to Give

### One-Time Donation

Make a single donation to support our work. Every contribution makes a difference.

### Monthly Giving

Become a regular supporter by setting up a monthly donation. Regular giving provides us with stable funding to plan long-term projects.

### Fundraising

Organize your own fundraising event or participate in one of ours. From sponsored runs to bake sales, there are many ways to raise funds.

### Legacy Giving

Remember ADHD UK in your will to leave a lasting legacy for future generations of people with ADHD.

### Corporate Partnerships

Partner with us through corporate sponsorship or workplace giving programs.

## Tax Benefits

As a registered charity, your donation may be eligible for Gift Aid, increasing its value at no extra cost to you.

## Make a Donation

To donate to ADHD UK, visit our [donation page](https://adhduk.co.uk/donate-to-adhd-uk/).

Thank you for your support in helping us improve the lives of people with ADHD across the UK.
