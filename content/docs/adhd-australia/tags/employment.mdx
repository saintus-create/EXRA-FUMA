The unique skills of employees with ADHD can help organisations break new ground if they're understood and harnessed. The ADHD Australia Employment team supports employers and employees to create workplaces that enable people with ADHD to perform at their best. They help redesign teams and re-craft jobs and roles to better reflect skills and capabilities.
