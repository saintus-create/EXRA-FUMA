## Coming Soon!

For many years, our community has told us two things:

- People living with ADHD, their families and supporters are searching for trustworthy, evidence-based resources.
- At the same time, incredible resource providers – teachers, clinicians, researchers, advocates, parents – often struggle to get their work seen.

That's why we're launching **ADHD Assist** – a new online platform, powered by [Neutopia](https://neutopia.co/?utm_source=adhd_australia), that brings these two worlds together.

ADHD Assist is a place to **discover, share and connect**. It's where resource providers can reach the people who need them most, and where our community can access practical, trustworthy support in one central hub.

This is about more than resources. It's about **building our ADHD Community**. By coming together with purpose, we not only make it easier for individuals to find support, we also shape the national conversation about ADHD – moving from awareness to real understanding.

ADHD Australia is stoked to launch ADHD Assist on **10 April, 2026**. We invite you to join us, explore the platform, and be part of writing the next chapter in Australia's ADHD.
