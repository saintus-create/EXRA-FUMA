ADHD Europe is headquartered in Belgium and functions as an international umbrella non-governmental organization that coordinates across various European national patient advocacy groups.

## Our Mission

The entity champions the standardization of access to diagnosis and treatment by leading policy initiatives, awareness campaigns, and informational exchanges at the European Union level.

## What We Do

### Policy Initiatives

ADHD Europe works at the EU level to:

- Advocate for consistent ADHD policies across member states
- Engage with European institutions and policymakers
- Promote EU-level recognition of ADHD needs
- Influence health and education policy frameworks

### Awareness Campaigns

We lead awareness campaigns to:

- Reduce stigma around ADHD across Europe
- Increase public understanding of ADHD
- Promote early identification and intervention
- Share success stories and positive narratives

### Informational Exchange

ADHD Europe facilitates:

- Sharing of best practices between national organizations
- Exchange of research and clinical information
- Collaboration on advocacy initiatives
- Networking among ADHD advocates across Europe

### Coordination of National Groups

As an umbrella organization, we:

- Connect national ADHD advocacy groups
- Support the development of new organizations
- Facilitate cross-border collaboration
- Amplify the collective voice of the European ADHD community

## Why ADHD Europe Matters

ADHD varies significantly across European countries in terms of:

- Access to diagnosis and treatment
- Educational support and accommodations
- Healthcare coverage and services
- Public awareness and understanding

ADHD Europe works to reduce these disparities and ensure consistent standards across the European Union.

## Our Impact

Through our work, we aim to:

- Standardize access to ADHD diagnosis and treatment
- Improve educational support for students with ADHD
- Ensure workplace accommodations for adults with ADHD
- Promote research and innovation in ADHD care
- Build a stronger European ADHD community
