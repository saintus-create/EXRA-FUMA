Students living with ADHD can thrive in an education environment that understands and supports them. The ADHD Australia Education Team supports students and educators with the tools and training that help them promote learning for ADHD students. Parents, family members, educators and students all have roles to play. Our goal is to promote the mutual understanding that will build a learning environment in which students with ADHD can thrive.
