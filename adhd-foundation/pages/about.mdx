The ADHD Foundation Neurodiversity Charity is a major neurodiversity-focused charity operating in the United Kingdom.

## Our Mission

The foundation provides direct screening services, early intervention support, and specialized training programs. The organization actively collaborates with academic institutions, primary and secondary schools, and commercial businesses to integrate neurodevelopmental support frameworks into educational and corporate environments.

## What We Offer

### Direct Screening Services

We provide ADHD screening services to:

- Identify individuals who may have ADHD
- Facilitate early diagnosis and intervention
- Reduce the time between symptom recognition and diagnosis
- Support individuals through the diagnostic process
- Provide guidance on next steps

### Early Intervention Support

Early intervention is crucial for ADHD. We offer:

- Support for newly diagnosed individuals and families
- Strategies for managing ADHD symptoms
- Educational support for children and young people
- Workplace guidance for adults
- Mental health and wellbeing support

### Specialized Training Programs

Our training programs include:

- Training for educators on supporting neurodiverse students
- Workplace training for employers and managers
- Parent training on supporting children with ADHD
- Healthcare professional training
- Community awareness training

### Collaboration with Institutions

We actively collaborate with:

- **Academic Institutions** - Research partnerships and evidence-based practice
- **Primary and Secondary Schools** - Integrating neurodevelopmental support
- **Commercial Businesses** - Creating neurodiverse-friendly workplaces
- **Healthcare Providers** - Improving ADHD identification and support
- **Community Organizations** - Building neurodiverse-friendly communities

## Our Approach

### Neurodiversity Focus

The ADHD Foundation takes a neurodiversity approach, recognizing that:

- ADHD is a natural variation in human neurology
- Neurodiverse individuals have unique strengths
- Society benefits from neurodiversity
- Support should focus on enabling success, not "fixing" individuals
- Inclusion benefits everyone

### Integrated Support Frameworks

We work to integrate neurodevelopmental support by:

- Creating comprehensive support systems
- Building capacity in organizations
- Developing sustainable support models
- Ensuring consistency across settings
- Promoting best practices

## Why the ADHD Foundation Matters

Early identification and support can:

- Improve educational outcomes
- Enhance employment prospects
- Reduce mental health challenges
- Strengthen family relationships
- Build self-esteem and confidence
